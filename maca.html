Our Heritage

A Legacy of
Purposeful
Enterprise

Founded in the heart of Tanzania, MACA has grown from a single trading venture into one of East Africa's most respected diversified holding companies. For nearly six decades, we have been building businesses that matter — creating jobs, developing infrastructure, and shaping industries.

Our approach is defined by long-term thinking, deep local knowledge, and an unwavering commitment to the people and communities we serve. We don't just invest in businesses — we invest in Tanzania's future.
